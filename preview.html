Brand Guidelines

Simple standards for a clean, consistent brand.

Use this page as the basic reference for Hawkinson Media’s logo, colors, typography, and everyday visual presentation.

View Brand Colors Logo Usage

Logo

The logo should feel clean, modern, and easy to read. Give it breathing room and avoid placing it on busy backgrounds.

HM Hawkinson Media
Clear space Keep open space around the logo equal to at least the height of the HM mark.

Minimum size Use the full logo only when it remains readable. Use the HM mark for small spaces.

Preferred background White, black, light gray, or brand blue backgrounds work best.

Colors

Keep the palette simple. Blue is the main accent. Gray supports text and secondary details. Black and white should carry most layouts.

Brand Blue #36ADFB
Brand Gray #808080
Black #0B0D10
White #FFFFFF

Typography

Rubik is the primary typeface. It should be used for headings, buttons, navigation, and body copy.

Rubik Extra Bold
Clean headings, strong labels, and readable navigation.
Use regular weight for body text, medium or bold for emphasis, and extra bold for major headlines. Avoid overusing all caps except for short labels or section tags.

Usage

These basic rules keep the brand from looking sloppy. Consistency matters more than decoration.

Do Keep it simple and readable.
  • Use strong contrast between text and background.
  • Use brand blue as an accent, not as the entire design.
  • Keep spacing generous and layouts uncluttered.
  • Use the same logo, colors, and font consistently.
Do Not Avoid weakening the brand.
  • Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo.
  • Do not place the logo on chaotic or low-contrast images.
  • Do not add random colors outside the brand palette.
  • Do not use multiple competing fonts in the same layout.
